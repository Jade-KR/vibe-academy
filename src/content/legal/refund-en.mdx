vibeAcademy ("we", "our", or "us") prioritizes student satisfaction. This refund policy has been established in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

## Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be requested under the following conditions:

- Students who have purchased a paid course
- The refund request is within the refund period specified below
- The course content contains critical errors that prevent normal learning

## Refund Period and Rates

The following refund rates apply based on the purchase date:

| Condition | Refund Period | Refund Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Within 7 days of purchase | 0% progress | Full refund (100%) |
| Within 7 days of purchase | Less than 30% progress | 70% refund |
| Within 7 days of purchase | 30% or more progress | No refund |
| After 7 days of purchase | Regardless of progress | No refund |

**Note**: Course progress is calculated as the ratio of completed lessons to total lessons.

## Refund Process

Refunds are processed as follows:

1. **Request**: Submit a refund request via the contact email below.
2. **Review**: We will confirm refund eligibility within 1-2 business days.
3. **Processing**: Once approved, the refund is issued to the original payment method.
4. **Completion**: Refunds are completed within 3-7 business days depending on the payment method.

## Refund Exceptions

Refunds may be restricted in the following cases:

- Course progress is 30% or more
- More than 7 days have passed since purchase
- Courses provided free through coupons or promotional discounts
- Products with separate refund conditions specified for events or special discounts
- Cases where course access sharing or transfer has been identified

## Subscription Refunds

For monthly/annual subscription products:

- **Cancellation**: You can cancel your subscription at any time. Service remains available until the end of the current billing period.
- **Partial Refunds**: Partial refunds for unused portions of the subscription period are not provided.
- **Annual Subscriptions**: Full refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if the service has not been used.

## Contact Information

For refund inquiries, please contact us:

- **Email**: support@vibeacademy.dev
- **Response Time**: Within 1-2 business days

## Policy Changes

This refund policy may be updated due to changes in applicable laws or service policies. Changes will be announced at least 7 days before they take effect.

- **Published**: February 3, 2026
